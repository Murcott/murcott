Chinese Pinyin Definition
悶騷 mensao A person who is outwardly cold but inwardly passionate.
羹湯 gen tang A type of thick soup, which usually uses starch to make the soup translucent and smooth. In the past, "geng" referred to any cooked soup or stew, with households serving it daily and for festive events (Wikipedia).
